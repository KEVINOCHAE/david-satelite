About Us

David’s Satellite Limited: We are a cutting-edge Tech company established in 2012. We take extreme pride in creating professional smart solutions that are designed based on innovative concepts and ideas. Utilizing the state-of-the-art technology, we provide most complete and integrated fleet monitoring solutions for transportation and logistics, enabling real-time visibility and control of transport assets as they move across the supply chain. In Addition, we are authorized Hikvision silver dealer partner and experienced CCTV & Access Control Security Systems service Provider. We are also an authorized Multichoice dealer in Aerial & Satellites Installation Services

With over 10 years of CCTV Security system, Aerial & Satellite services and Fleet Management systems integration experience in our chosen markets, we have provided total solutions to many corporate clients ranging from logistics Companies, industrial clients, banks to smaller startup businesses. Many of these projects required specialized integration between differing products, which when utilizing our extensive expertise and experience in hardware and software integration, we deliver custom solutions based on clients requirements with precision and unbeaten data accuracy.

David’s Satellite Fleet system is an asset monitoring and management platform/application based on the world leading Telematics platform Wialon or GPS gate. Whether you simply want to have visibility into your fleet or asset or you want to do sophisticated analytics, the platform can help you. The interface is designed to be simple yet provide all pinpointed requirement by the most demanding user. You get only the information that you need to help in faster decision-making. The user can add or remove any of the workbench windows depending on the need. Many users just use the notification pane for alerting and then add the other windows for real time monitoring. Servicing our client‘s needs is our prime focus, and this is one of the key areas which have contributed to our success. We believe that if we have satisfied clients, they represent the best marketing option available.
